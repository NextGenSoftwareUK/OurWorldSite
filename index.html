THE VISION

A NEW KIND OF GAME

Our World is not just a game — it's a movement. Built on the OASIS platform and powered by OGEngine, it overlays a rich digital world onto the real one using augmented and virtual reality, anchored to real GPS coordinates around the globe.

Every location you visit, every positive action you take — planting trees, helping neighbours, volunteering, reducing waste — earns Karma. Karma is your currency, your reputation, and your power in Our World. The better you are in the real world, the stronger you become in the digital one.

Quests, achievements and inventory are shared across the entire OASIS Omniverse via OGEngine — complete a mission in ODOOM, carry the rewards into Our World. One unified identity. One interconnected universe.
